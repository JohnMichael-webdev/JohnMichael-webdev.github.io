Great Wall of China

One of the longest structures ever built, the Great Wall stretches over 21,000 km to protect China's borders.

Christ the Redeemer

This massive statue of Jesus Christ overlooks Rio de Janeiro and symbolizes peace and openness.

Machu Picchu

An Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains, known for its sophisticated dry-stone construction.

Chichen Itza

A large Mayan city on Mexico's Yucatán Peninsula, famed for the Temple of Kukulcán pyramid.

Roman Colosseum

A Roman amphitheater that once held 50,000 spectators, famous for its gladiator battles and shows.

Taj Mahal

A white marble mausoleum in India built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Petra

An archaeological city in Jordan famous for its rock-cut architecture and rose-colored stone cliffs.
