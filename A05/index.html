About

Manila Ocean Park is the Philippines’ first world-class marine theme park and oceanarium. It features interactive marine and wildlife exhibits, thrilling shows, and unique experiences for visitors of all ages.

Top Attractions

Oceanarium

Walk through a 25-meter tunnel surrounded by thousands of marine creatures.

Sea Lion Show

Enjoy the playful tricks and talents of trained sea lions in this family-favorite show.

Trails to Antarctica

Discover icy fun with penguins, snow play, and slides in a sub-zero adventure zone.
