Genshin Characters Description
Furina

She is the Hydro Archon of Fontaine, one of the game's regions, and is known for her elegance and beauty. As the ruler of Fontaine, Furina is deeply involved in the justice system of her region, where she values law and order. She also has a reputation for being enigmatic, balancing her duties as an archon with her personal ideals.

Venti

Venti is the playful and carefree Anemo Archon who governs Mondstadt, the City of Freedom. Known for his love of music and wine, he often appears in the guise of a wandering bard. Despite his lighthearted demeanor, Venti holds deep wisdom and strength, and he plays a vital role in protecting Mondstadt's freedom.

Zhongli

Zhongli is the Geo Archon of Liyue, known for his wisdom, elegance, and deep respect for tradition. In his mortal guise, he works as a consultant for the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor, offering profound knowledge of history and contracts. Despite his calm and composed demeanor, Zhongli possesses immense strength and an unshakable sense of duty to Liyue.

Nahida

Nahida is the Dendro Archon of Sumeru. Despite her youthful appearance, she is highly intelligent and possesses immense wisdom. As the vessel of Lesser Lord Kusanali, she plays a crucial role in guiding Sumeru and uncovering its hidden truths.
